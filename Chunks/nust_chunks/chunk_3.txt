2. Campuses
NUST operates a multi-campus system across Pakistan. Its main campus is located in Islamabad, and 5 campuses in total.
Campus | Location
Islamabad | Sector H-12, Islamabad, Pakistan
Rawalpindi Campus | Military College of Signals (MCS), Rawalpindi
Risalpur Campus | College of Aeronautical Engineering (CAE), Risalpur
Karachi Campus | Pakistan Navy Engineering College (PNEC), Karachi
Quetta Campus | College of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (CEME) regional extension
