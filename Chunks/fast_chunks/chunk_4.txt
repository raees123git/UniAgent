2. Campuses
FAST‑NUCES have 5 campuses across Pakistan. The main campus is Islamabad campus:
Campus | Location / Notes
Islamabad | Sector H-11 / H-12, Islamabad — original and one of the primary campuses.
Lahore Campus | Lahore — large campus with extensive undergraduate and graduate programmes.
Karachi Campus | Karachi — strong industry links to local tech firms.
Peshawar Campus | Peshawar — expanding programmes in computing and engineering.
