3.2 MS (eligibility criteria)
eligibility criteria for admission in fast is as follows:
• 16-year degree (BS/BE or equivalent) in a relevant discipline from an HEC-recognized institution.
• Minimum CGPA requirements (often around 2.5/4.0) may apply; specific departments set their own thresholds.
• Graduate applicants usually required to take GAT (General) / GRE and may attend interviews.
